I'm an assistant professor at Shanghai Center for Mathematical Science (SCMS), Fudan University. Before joining Fudan, I obtained my doctorate degree from Duke University and my bachelor's degree from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

Research interests (on a coarse grid): optimization, mathematical statistics, applied harmonic analysis, operations research, machine learning

Research interests (on a fine grid, roughly in descending order of recent time devotion, as of Oct 2022): zeroth-order optimization, stochastic optimization, connections between analytic properties of measures and statistical properties of distributions, lower and upper bounds of some stochastic processes in operations research and machine learning, bandit learning problems

I'm also interested in deep learning, but haven't found a good point to jump in yet...
