Tianyu Wang

I'm an assistant professor at Shanghai Center for Mathematical Science (SCMS), Fudan University. Before joining Fudan, I obtained my doctorate degree from Duke University. My research interests include computational mathematics, optimization, machine learning, and data-driven decision making. Currently, my primary focus is zeroth-order optimization and related computational problems.
